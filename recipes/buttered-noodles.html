Simple Buttered Noodles

A simple recipe for buttered noodles. It just needs butter, spices, and noodles to make it. This is a good recipe if you have limited resources. You can add more ingredients as you can see fit. It makes for a good side for other dishes and is also great on its own

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 1 lb pasta (fettuccine or spaghetti)
  • herbs and cheese as needed

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to box directions
  2. Place butter in a large saucepan to skillet and melt over medium heat. Add minced garlic and stur until fragrant (30-45 seconds)
  3. Turn off heat and stir in salt and pepper
  4. Pour over sauce over pasta
  5. Optional sprinkle with grated Parmesan and parsley
